How to install

Instalation process depends on your OS. Below you can see instructions for Linux, Windows and Macintosh:

Linux

Drop downloaded ImageCache.png to ~/.audacity-data/Theme, in Audacity open Preferences and choose Custom theme under Interface tab.

Windows

Drop downloaded ImageCache.png to %appdata%/audacity/Theme, in Audacity open Preferences and choose Custom theme under Interface tab.

Macintosh

Drop downloaded ImageCache.png to ~/Library/Application Support/audacity/Theme, in Audacity open Preferences and choose Custom theme under Interface tab.
